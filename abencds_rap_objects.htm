ABAP CDS - RAP Objects

CDS RAP objects are special CDS objects that support ABAP RAP:

  • Service definitions for RAP Business Services (BS)
  • Behavior definitions for RAP Business Objects (BO)

More information about ABAP RAP can be found under ABAP RESTful Programming Model.
