operator

Component of an ABAP statement or of a CDS data definition that, when combined with operands, forms an expression. When the statement is executed, an operation is executed for two operands connected by one operator, and the result is processed further in the statement. There are arithmetic, bit, string, Boolean, relational, and assignment operators. There are also operators that do not link operands, but perform other operations in operand positions. Operators of this type are the dereferencing operator, declarations operators, and constructor operators. Finally, the literal operator is used to link two literals.
