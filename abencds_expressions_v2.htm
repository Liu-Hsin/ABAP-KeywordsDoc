CDS DDL - CDS View Entity, Expressions

Syntax

... arith_expr
  | aggr_expr
  | case_expr
  | cast_expr
  | func ...

Effect

CDS DDL expressions are expressions that can be specified in a CDS DDL operand position of a CDS view entity.

The following CDS DDL expressions exist:

  • Arithmetic expressions
  • Aggregate expressions
  • Case distinctions
  • Cast expressions
  • Calls of built-in CDS functions
