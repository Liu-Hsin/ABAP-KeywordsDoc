ABAP CDS - Service Definitions

A CDS service definition exposes CDS entities for a business service. Service definitions are created in SDL source code using CDS SDL.

  • SDL for Service Definitions

Service definitions can expose regular CDS entities and root entities of RAP business objects:

  • The publication of a regular CDS entity as a business server enables queries to be made from outside ABAP on the associated data using the protocol from the service binding.
  • The publication of root entities of RAP business objects also enables transactional writes to be made on the associated data.
