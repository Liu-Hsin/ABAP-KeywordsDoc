child entity

Each CDS entity of a CDS composition tree or of the tree-like hierarchical structure of a RAP business object other than the root entity, is a child entity. A child entity is defined as the composition target of a CDS composition in its parent entity and must contain a to-parent association with its parent entity. A child entity can have exactly one parent entity.
